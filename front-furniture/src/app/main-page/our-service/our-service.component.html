Manufacturing Doors

Molded doors are constructed by pressing or molding two panels together to form a particular style. Molded doors are available in both a smooth or textured surface and come pre-primed. These doors are ready for painting, but because they are primed, they cannot be stained. Opposite of molded doors are flush doors.

Manufacturing Bed

Manufacturing Sofa
